## RTX 6090 / Nvidia Joke
**Best fit:** Ch 4 (The Arms Race) or Ch 1 (The Great Normalization — financial circus section)

The RTX 6090 is coming — and yes, it's hilarious because it's 69. But beyond the juvenile (and correct) humor, there's a real angle here: Nvidia's data centers run on specialized hardware (H100s, B200s, etc.), not consumer GPUs. But that doesn't stop every LinkedIn thought leader from posting about how "AI will work more better if we just wire up some more 6090s." The consumer GPU branding has become a proxy for the entire AI infrastructure conversation, even though the actual relationship between your gaming rig and the data center training GPT-5 is approximately zero. The joke writes itself: the solution to every AI problem is apparently just more Nvidia cards, stacked higher, running hotter, in a warehouse somewhere in Iowa.
